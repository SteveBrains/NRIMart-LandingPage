Everything you need to know about NRIMart

What items can I order through NRIMart? +
You can order almost anything available in India ‚Äì groceries, spices, snacks, medicines (with prescription), clothing, handicrafts, electronics, and even items from Amazon India or Flipkart.
How long does shipping take? +
Delivery times vary by destination: USA/Canada: 7-14 days, UK/Europe: 8-12 days, UAE/Middle East: 5-10 days, Australia/NZ: 10-15 days. Express shipping options are available.
How are the fees calculated? +
Our pricing includes: Product cost + Service fee (5-10%) + Case Partner fee + Packaging charges + Shipping (weight √ó per-kg rate). You'll see the complete breakdown before placing your order.
Can I track my parcel? +
Absolutely! Every parcel gets a unique tracking number and QR code. You can track your order in real-time from purchase to delivery. We also send photo and video updates at key stages.
What if my items arrive damaged? +
We take great care in packaging, but in the rare case of damage, you're covered. All parcels include basic protection, and optional premium insurance is available for valuable items.
How do I become a Case Partner? +
Apply through our Partner section! Requirements include: Indian residency, smartphone with internet, bank account, and willingness to help customers. After verification and training, you can start earning.
